Effective Date: March 2026

Welcome to Hubly AI! Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous analytics data regarding how you use the App to improve the user experience.
  • Local Storage: The App stores your "Favorites" and "Recent History" directly on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Device Information: We may collect non-personal device information for diagnostic and debugging purposes.
  • Account Information: If you create an account via Firebase Authentication, we store your basic account information securely on Firebase to provide cross-device synchronization.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob: Used to serve advertisements within the App. AdMob may use device identifiers to serve personalized ads.
  • Firebase & Google Analytics: Used for crash reporting, authentication, and understanding user interactions.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize your experience.
  • To serve relevant advertisements.
  • To monitor App performance and fix crashes.

4. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us with your personal information. We rely on Google's Firebase secure infrastructure. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Children’s Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us
